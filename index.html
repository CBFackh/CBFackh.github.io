Projects

Ein Fuchs im Netz

"Ein Fuchs im Netz" (Finn goes Online) is a point-and-click adventure for mobile games, teaching children about the topics of cybersecurity and prepares them for the dangers on the internet. The player accompanies the cyber fox Finn, who has crash-landed on Earth with his spaceship. The goal is to help Finn repair his spaceship so that he can continue his travels in space.

The game also can be expanded using a MicroBit or Calliope mini-computer.

I joined the project in a later stage of development. My initial responsibility was to port the game to iOS and Android devices. Later, as the previous lead developer left, I took over the role as lead developer and coordinated the work of three developer, including code review and mentoring.

Tools I used: Unity3D, Unity Cloud Build, Unity Analytics, JetBrains Rider, GitLab, Git Terminal, Confluence/JIRA, Android Studio, XCode.

VerstehMal: Das Wasser

"Versteh Mal: Das Wasser" by peachy360 is an educational app for children in primary school about the hydrologic cycle. The story of the App develops in handdrawn scenes the player colors. When he`s done, the painting gets animated and the main characters, two curious water drops, can continue their journey.

In the team of seven artist, developer and designer I was one of two developer. I was in charge of the application architecture, core gameplay mechanics and editor-tools. Further more I took over the role as team lead and coordinated the agile development process.

Tools I used: Unity3D, Unity Cloud Build, Unity Analytics, VS Code, GitLab, Git Terminal, Confluence/JIRA, XCode.
