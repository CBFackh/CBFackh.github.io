Projects

Ein Fuchs im Netzphone_iphone

Trailer Android iOS

"Ein Fuchs im Netz" is a point-and-click adventure for mobile games, teaching children about cybersecurity and preparing them for the dangers on the internet. The player helps the cyber-fox Finn, who has crash-landed on Earth, to repair his spaceship so that he can continue his travels in space.

The game can also be expanded using a Micro:Bit or Calliope mini-computer.

Joining the project in a later stage of development, I was in charge of porting the app to iOS and Android devices. Later I took over the role as lead developer and coordinated the work of three developers, including code review and mentoring.

Tools used: Unity3D, Unity Cloud Build, Unity Analytics, JetBrains Rider, GitLab, Git Terminal, Confluence/JIRA, Android Studio, XCode

The Great Sassanellicomputer

Trailer

"The Great Sassanelli" is a text-based, illustrated narrative game for PC and Mac about managing circus in WWI Europe. The player travels with the circuss across Europe, hires performers, entertains cities and villages, keeps the circus safe and seeks sanctuary.

As sole programmer I was in charge of developing a prototype that could be used to look for publisher and investors. The core part was the implementation of the Inkle Engine into Unity3D.

Tools used: Unity3D, Inkle, JetBrains Rider, GitLab, Confluence/JIRA

The Fold-Lab, a companion app to the FOLDIO Coding Kits, allows children to program their Micro:Bit v2 using simple block-coding on the phone and to transfer the created programs to the Micro:Bit via bluetooth.

VerstehMal: Das Wasserphone_iphone

Trailer Android

"Versteh Mal: Das Wasser" by peachy360 is an educational app for children in primary school about the hydrologic cycle. The story consists of eight coloring scenes, the player fills with color. When he's done, the painting gets animated and the main characters, two curious water drops, can continue their journey.

In the team of seven artists, developers and designers I was one of two developers. I was in charge of planning and executing the applications architecture, core gameplay mechanics and editor tools. Furthermore, I took over the role as team lead and coordinated the agile development process.

Tools used: Unity3D, Unity Cloud Build, Unity Analytics, VS Code, GitLab, Git Terminal, Confluence/JIRA, XCode
